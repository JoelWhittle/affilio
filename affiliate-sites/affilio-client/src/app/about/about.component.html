About Mays Bunnays

Welcome to Mays Bunnays, your ultimate guide to all things rabbit care! Based in Blackpool, England, we’re here to help you give your bunnies the love, care, and comfort they deserve.

Our Story

At Mays Bunnays, our journey started with a simple idea: to create a space where rabbit owners can find everything they need to keep their bunnies happy and healthy. From expert advice to curated product recommendations, we’re passionate about making rabbit care easy, fun, and accessible for everyone.

Our Values

Rabbits are more than pets—they’re family! That’s why we prioritize high-quality, trustworthy advice and handpicked product recommendations. We believe every rabbit deserves the best, and we’re here to make that happen for yours.
